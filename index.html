A Tinder App for Finding Studs

Are you a broodmare searching for your perfect sire? Look no further with Studmate--a mobile app for locating stallions that are DTM (down to mate). Our Tinder-esque platform makes browsing your next match easier than ever.

Claire has been riding horses for over 10 years. In her experience, she's witnessed a huge gap in technological solutions within the Equine Industry (which has annual revenues upwards of $39 billion). Claire developed the Studmate app as a fun alternative to sites like Stallions Now, which features Tinder-like swiping through potential matches of stallions standing at stud.
